Hi, I'm Christopher Grady

I'm a full stack web developer and graduate of Thinkful's Full Stack Engineering program, residing in San Diego, CA, with my wonderful girlfriend and our crazy cats.

I'm looking to build my skills to have the ability to create interesting, useful sites and apps that are both intellectually stimulating and aesthetically pleasing.

I'm a passionate, hard worker, always looking to refine my craft, as years in the film industry have taught me you're either growing, or you're falling behind. So get busy living or get busy dying.

About Me

I grew up with the internet, designing pages on GeoCities (remember that?), and the web holds a very dear place in my heart. Growing up in a very small town, I didn't have access to much educational material. The web changed all that. I could find the guitar tablature for songs I wanted to learn immediately. I watched Wikipedia grow. I am forever grateful for all the information the web has provided me.

My years in the film industry taught me flexibility and the value of communication and collaboration. I would like to use these skills to aid me in designing websites that truly display the client's vision, that are unique and packed with personality.

Services I provide:

  • Create engaging sites that showcase the client's vision.
  • Use JavaScript to enhance interactivity.
  • Consult on design, to create an aesthetically pleasing experience for the user.

Oh, and I'm a retro gaming enthusiast. Raspberry Pi is love.
